who is this for?

creating a ghost is one form: a name, an optional photo (blurred forever), and the platform you knew them on.

09

what ghost is, and isn't.

ghost is a private writing app. not therapy, not counseling, not a substitute for talking to someone who can help. if you're in crisis, please reach out — 988 (us) or findahelpline.com (global).

imagined replies are fictional. assembled locally from word banks and clearly labeled "imagined". the real person is never contacted, never aware, never involved. nothing is sent to anyone, ever.

your data stays on the phone. no servers, no analytics, no telemetry, no tracking. ghost cannot read what you wrote — there is no "ghost" reading on the other side, only your own device.

export is optional. save any thread to a still image kept in your photo library. ghost never auto-posts, never shares, never syncs. if a thread leaves the app, you put it there.
