01what ghost is

ghost is a private journaling app for the apple ecosystem. it gives you a sealed surface to write messages that are never sent to anyone — words you'd say to a person you've decided not to (or can't) actually message. nothing you write inside ghost reaches another human being through ghost. the recipient side of every conversation is fictional or empty.

02what ghost is not

  • ghost is not a messaging service. you cannot send a message to anyone using this app.
  • ghost is not therapy, counseling, or a medical service. it is a writing tool. if you are in crisis, please reach out to 988 (us) or findahelpline.com (global).
  • ghost is not a social network. there is no feed, no follow, no public profile, no community feature, no comments, no upvotes.
  • ghost is not a "deceased loved ones" simulator and should not be used to grieve people who have died. the imagined-reply engine is a deliberately playful word-bank assembly with explicit fiction labels — it is not designed to comfort or console as a substitute for the real person.
